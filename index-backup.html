About Alana Hogg
Digital Design
Specializing in websites, web banner ads, social media graphics, eblasts, interactive presentations, e-learning solutions, ecommerce and more. I also have experience with standards-based HTML5 and Bootstrap, CSS, WordPress CMS, Concrete5 CMS, SiteCake CMS, Squarespace, Adobe Business Catalyst CMS, Drupal, Ekklesia360 CMS, MailChimp, CheetahMail, Emma and Constant Contact.
